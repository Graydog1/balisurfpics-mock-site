About us here at BALISURFPICS

Here at Balisurfpics we want to make sure that you get to take home those quality shots from those memorable holiday surfs! We use only top end camera gear which means all the images that you take away are of very high quality and can be used at any size.

All of our photographers here at Balisurfpics have spent an extensive time on the island, this means we are able to help point you in the right direction for the best waves for your ability on the day of shooting. If there is a certain kind of wave that you are after, we will be able to find it!
